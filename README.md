# FanDecode

Turn YouTube comments into actionable audience insights.

## Overview

FanDecode is a YouTube comment analysis platform for creators, marketers, and content teams.  
It helps you move from raw comments to clear decisions by organizing feedback into structured insights.

## Why It Matters

YouTube comments contain direct audience signals, but manual review is slow and inconsistent at scale.  
FanDecode helps you quickly understand what viewers are feeling, asking, and repeatedly mentioning so you can improve content strategy with confidence.

## How FanDecode Handles YouTube Comment Analysis

FanDecode is built specifically for this workflow:

- Classifies comments into meaningful intent categories
- Summarizes audience sentiment
- Extracts recurring themes and top viewer questions
- Tracks analyses through a job-based workflow
- Supports channel-level history for ongoing trend analysis
- Lets you inspect detailed outputs for transparency

## What Makes FanDecode Different

Most tools provide generic summaries. FanDecode is designed for creator decision-making:

- Focused on YouTube comment intelligence, not general-purpose text summarization
- Combines high-level insights with drill-down visibility
- Connects individual analyses with channel-level context over time
- Built for repeatable workflows that support publishing decisions

## Who It’s For

- YouTube creators
- Content strategists
- Marketing teams
- Agencies managing multiple channels

## Use Cases

- Identify recurring viewer complaints after a video launch
- Detect sentiment shifts across uploads
- Collect common audience questions for follow-up content
- Prioritize next-video topics based on real viewer demand
